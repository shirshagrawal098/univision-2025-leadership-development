Projects – UniVision 2025–26

Projects provide the practical application competence to the participants, allowing them to work collaboratively, employ innovation mindset, and use structured methods to solve the identified real problems on the ground and institute change. This instils the confidence and provides them with the experience to potentially take on any challenges in the future.

Faculty Industry Engagement and Training/Internships

To strengthen ties with industry and keep our faculty aligned with the evolving business landscape, this project provides immersive industry exposure and collaboration opportunities. It encourages internships, industry visits, and co-mentorship to blend theory with practice.

Employee Communications – Townhalls & Reports

Through structured communication channels like quarterly townhalls, campus reports, and annual gatherings, this project ensures every employee is aligned with the university’s mission, progress, and achievements—fostering unity and shared vision.

Employee Experience and Engagement

This project reimagines the employee journey, focusing on enriching daily experiences, onboarding, recognition, and wellness. By nurturing a sense of belonging and joy, we aim to create a vibrant and supportive work environment.

School and Department-wise Result Analysis and Audits

Using structured audits and deep analysis of academic results, this project identifies patterns, gaps, and growth opportunities. It empowers schools and departments to implement meaningful improvements at the program and subject level.

Student Experience and Success at Department Level

This initiative enhances the connection between departments and their students. Through proactive mentoring, targeted interventions, and engagement programs, it strengthens students’ academic journeys and personal growth.

External Collaborations Management

Focused on managing MOUs and academic-industry collaborations, this project brings structure, accountability, and strategic follow-up to our external partnerships. It defines measurable outcomes to ensure lasting value.

CATS Operations and Inventory Management

This initiative enhances the operational efficiency of campus tech (CATS). From inventory tracking to deployment scheduling, it ensures that the right tools reach the right hands, enabling smoother teaching, learning, and administration.

Campus Security and Facilities Improvement

Through safety upgrades, maintenance reviews, and infrastructure audits, this project champions a safe, secure, and accessible campus for all. It reflects our commitment to continuous improvement of the physical environment.

Academic Processes and Reporting

This project aims to improve internal academic workflows by automating reports, standardizing documentation, and reducing redundancies. The result? Faster decisions, greater accountability, and better academic governance.

ProVC Shadow Board

By forming a ProVC Shadow Board of emerging leaders, this initiative fosters decision-making exposure, leadership grooming, and succession planning. It also provides real-time feedback and reflection on strategic initiatives.
