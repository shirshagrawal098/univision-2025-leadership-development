About UniVision 2025

Nurturing transformative academic leadership for GITAM

Our Purpose

UniVision 2025 is a transformative leadership development program launched by the Centre for Academic Innovation and Advancement (CAIA) at GITAM University, Bengaluru Campus. This initiative is designed to develop future leaders of the university by fostering exceptional academic leadership.

Through this program, we aim to cultivate a new generation of academic and administrative leaders who will drive innovation, excellence, and positive change in higher education.
