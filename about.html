About UniVision 2025

Nurturing transformative academic leadership for GITAM's Vision 2040

Our Purpose

UniVision 2025 is a transformative leadership development program launched by the Centre for Academic Innovation and Advancement (CAIA) at GITAM University, Bengaluru Campus. This initiative is designed to support the institution's Vision 2040 by fostering exceptional academic leadership.

Through this program, we aim to cultivate a new generation of academic leaders who will drive innovation, excellence, and positive change in higher education.

Why Leadership in Academia Matters

Innovation

Strong leadership drives academic innovation and keeps institutions at the forefront of educational excellence.

Community

Effective leaders build strong academic communities that foster collaboration and growth.

Growth

Leadership development ensures sustainable institutional growth and adaptation to changing educational landscapes.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To develop and nurture transformative academic leaders who will drive GITAM's journey towards Vision 2040.

Our Vision

To be the catalyst for excellence in academic leadership, fostering innovation and positive change in higher education.
